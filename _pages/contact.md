We value feedback and inquiries from our users. Whether you have a question about our text-to-speech tools, need help with a tutorial, or are interested in a partnership, please get in touch.

## General Inquiries
For general questions or support, please email us at:
**Email:** [soundtext@appz.se](mailto:soundtext@appz.se)

## Technical Support
If you encounter issues while using Sound of Text, please include the following details in your email to help us assist you faster:
* The text you were trying to convert.
* The language/accent selected.
* The device and browser you are using.
