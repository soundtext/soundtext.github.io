Sound of Text is committed to providing high-quality, trustworthy, and helpful content to our users. Our editorial guidelines ensure that every piece of information we publish meets the highest standards of integrity and expertise.

## Expertise and Experience
Our content is written by a team of specialists with first-hand experience in audio technology, digital accessibility, and content creation. We don't just report on tools; we use them. Every tutorial is based on actual testing and verification of the software and platforms mentioned.

## Accuracy and Fact-Checking
We strive for 100% accuracy in all our guides. Before publication, our editors verify all technical steps, language support claims, and platform compatibility. We regularly update our content to reflect the latest changes in the TTS landscape and platform updates (like WhatsApp or TikTok).

## Trust and Transparency
Transparency is core to our mission. If a tool or service we review has limitations, we state them clearly. Our goal is to help you find the *right* solution, not just any solution. 

## No AI-Generated Content
While we provide tools for AI-driven voice synthesis, our editorial content—the articles, guides, and reviews—is strictly human-authored. We believe that human insight and experience are irreplaceable when it comes to providing helpful advice.

## Correction Policy
If you find an error in any of our articles, please contact us at [soundtext@appz.se](mailto:soundtext@appz.se). We investigate all reports and issue corrections promptly to ensure our readers have the most accurate information available.
