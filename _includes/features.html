How Can Sound of Text Help You?

Sound of Text is a free, accessible web-based Text-to-Speech tool that converts written text into natural-sounding audio from Technolati ID. Whether you're creating content, learning a new language, or making your work more accessible, our platform has you covered.

Content Creation

Generate high-quality voiceovers for podcasts and videos with our complete text-to-speech guide — no hardware required.

Education & Learning

Create audio from study notes, textbooks, or flashcards — perfect for learning on the go with our educational tools tutorial.

WhatsApp & Messaging

Craft fun, personalized voice messages for WhatsApp, Telegram, and other messaging apps in any language. Learn how to set it up here.

Social Media & TikTok

Create viral audio for TikTok videos, Instagram Reels, and other social media — no microphone or voice actor needed.

Accessibility

Convert written articles, documents, and blog posts into spoken audio for easier consumption by everyone.

Developer Integration

Use our Text to Speech API to integrate voice-generation capabilities directly into your own apps and websites.
